h2. Catalog Sources

h4. NEIC PDE

This is the catalog of the United States Geological Survey. "More information &raquo;":http://earthquake.usgs.gov/research/data/pde.php
 
h4. ISC

Bulletin of the International Seismological Centre (ISC). A list of earthquakes two years behind real time. "More information &raquo;":http://www.isc.ac.uk/
 
h4. ANF/ANFR

Earthquake locations published by the USArray "Array Network Facility":http://anf.ucsd.edu/ (ANF). The ANF also publishes event phase readings which are available from the DMC. The @R@ in the ANFR catalog indicates the final *Reviewed* publication.

h4. UofW (University of Washington)

A research catalog of phase picks from the University of Washington. "More information &raquo;":http://www.pnsn.org/
